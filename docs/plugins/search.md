## grug-far
Provides search and replace across files. It is wired with keymaps under `leader s`.

## flash
Adds fast jump motions (like a more powerful `f`).

## harpoon
Lets you mark files and jump between them quickly.

## trouble
Shows diagnostics and lists in a dedicated panel.
