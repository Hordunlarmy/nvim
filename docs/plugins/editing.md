## Comment.nvim
Adds `gc` mappings for commenting lines and blocks. Works in visual mode as well.

## mini.surround
Provides `gs` mappings to add, delete, replace, and find surroundings.

## mini.ai
Adds extra text objects for functions, classes, blocks, and tags.

## paredit
Helps structural editing for Lisp-style languages.

## autopairs
Automatically inserts matching pairs for quotes/brackets.

## yanky
Improves yank/paste history and navigation.

## vim-sleuth and indent-omatic
Automatically detects indentation settings based on file content.

## indent-blankline
Shows indentation guides for clarity.

## vim-matchup
Enhances `%` motion and provides better matching pair navigation.
